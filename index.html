FAQ'S

Quick answers to common questions.

Is it actually real melon?+

Absolutely! Every Melonthies smoothie is made with real, fresh watermelon as the star ingredient, blended with carefully selected fruits and natural ingredients for a refreshing, authentic taste.

Where do you deliver?+

We're starting within Lagos. Message us your address on WhatsApp and we'll confirm if you're in range, most orders arrive in under an hour.

Why only 6 flavors?+

We've intentionally curated six signature flavors to ensure each one is perfectly crafted, consistently delicious, and made with the freshest ingredients. Quality always comes before quantity.

Can I order before August 3rd?+

Not just yet. Our official launch is on August 3rd, and orders will open from that date. We can't wait to serve you!

What sizes do you offer?+

Melonthies smoothies are available in 350ml and 500ml bottles, so you can choose the perfect size whether you're looking for a quick refresh or a longer-lasting treat.
