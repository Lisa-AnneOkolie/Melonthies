The Ritual

How to build your routine

A simple order to follow so every product does its job — from cleansing to sealing in moisture.

Step One

Cleanse

Massage the Shampoo into wet hair and scalp for 1–2 minutes, then rinse thoroughly to lift buildup without stripping moisture.

Step Two

Condition

Apply the Rinse-Out Conditioner mid-length to ends, leave for 3–5 minutes, then rinse. Follow with the Hair Mask 1–2x weekly for deep repair.

Step Three

Nourish

On damp hair, smooth in the Leave-in Conditioner, then work the Hair Booster Cream through length and ends to lock in moisture.

Step Four

Stimulate & Protect

Massage the Hair Booster Oil into your scalp daily or every other day. Finish with the Anti-Dandruff Spray as needed to keep your scalp balanced.
